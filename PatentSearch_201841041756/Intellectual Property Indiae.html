Abstract:

The present invention overcomes the drawback of the technology models available in the state of the art by providing an smart docking system, wherein the smart docking system is compatible with wide variety of vehicles and vehicle can be converted into sharing vehicle without making any modifications to vehicle body . The present invention is also capable of recharging the onboard battery of electrically powered vehicle . The present invention provides a better solution than Current docking systems for vehicles. Current docking systems are vulnerable to vandalism and inconvenient for user"s. Also ,one has to alter the body of vehicle and in addition to that size of vehicle must be compatible with the docking station otherwise locking action will not take place .hence, the current docking system is unable to function with wide variety of vehicle and can only work with well customized vehicle according to docking system"s locking mechanism.
