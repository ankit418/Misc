Abstract:

A driving system for controlling the speed of a wheelchair A driving system for a powered wheelchair (100) comprising a hand lever (101), a prime mover (102), and a two-speed gearbox (103). The prime mover (102) is connected to the hand lever (101) and the two-speed gearbox (103) is connected to the prime mover (102), so that when the hand lever (101), is pushed by an user of the wheelchair(100), the rotational movement of an input shaft (112) is transferred to an output shaft (113) through a first pinion gear (107) and a first gear (109) such that a male dog clutch (105) and a female dog clutch (106) are engaged and a relative movement between the output shaft (113) and a freewheel (111) allows the freewheel (111) to rotate in an anticlockwise direction thereby providing lesser gear ratio in the first gear (109) to induce low torque on a wheel shaft (100a) of the wheelchair (100).
